## Parking

### 515

On Sunday mornings, anyone without bulkier gear will be shuttling in from the Rollins parking lot @ 2170 Piedmont Rd NE, Atlanta GA 30324. Shuttles will pick up from a well-marked area near the center of the lot. You are welcome to walk, but shuttles will run frequently.

*For those with gear, you will receive a weekly code to park in the backlot.*

### CBL

On Sundays, please park in the JE DUNN lot, as marked on the map in yellow.

**JE DUNN ADDRESS:** 2555 Cumberland Pkwy SE

There is a walkway connecting JE Dunn and the CBL lot (refer to map).

### TRL

Band parking is located in the gravel lot next to the Town of Trilith adjacent to TRL Live's loading dock. If facing the main front entrance of TRL Live, parking will be to the right of the building.

**175 Trilith Parkway, Fayetteville, GA 30214**

*This address may lead you to the parking deck side. You need to exit the roundabout to head towards the right of TRL Live's main entrance and towards the Town of Trilith to get to the gravel lot adjacent to TRL Live.*
