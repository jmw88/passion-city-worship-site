## Compensation

Once you begin serving in our house, apart from Bloom or choir, you will be compensated starting from your 5th confirmed date. This includes shadow dates. Compensation is a small way for us to honor and value the gift you are bringing to our house, as well as the time and effort you put into practicing and preparing these songs.

If you have any questions, please email [worship@passioncitychurch.com](mailto:worship@passioncitychurch.com).
