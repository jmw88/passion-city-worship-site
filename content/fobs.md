## FOBs / Credentials

You will be given a fob to access the environments you'll need to be in when serving. Having a fob is a privilege — getting to do what we do and having access to the spaces we are in — so we ask that you use discernment when using it as well as knowing when not to use it (not being in the spaces when you don't need to be, etc.). Thank you for your continued cooperation in treating what we have access to with respect.

Should anyone be found overusing or abusing the privilege of having a fob, we and other teams reserve the right to revoke fob access and privileges. Abuse would consist of, but not limited to, the following examples:

- Using it on weeks when you're not rostered.
- Allowing entry through doors to others without permission.
- If you're rostered in the morning (Students, Kids, TRL or AM AUD teams) but not in the evenings and use it in the evenings.

If you have any fob questions, reach out to Chris Donovan at [chris.donovan@passioncitychurch.com](mailto:chris.donovan@passioncitychurch.com).
