## Green Room / Guest Lounge

Before gatherings, we gather together to eat, build community, and pray in what is called the "green room or guest lounge." The purpose of the green room is primarily for those things and not a place to simply hang out.

We encourage our team to remain accessible and visible in places like the Oval at 515 or Atrium at CBL and throughout other parts of our building, not just in the green room. Because of this and wanting to be present in the gatherings, we discourage our team from being in the green room all day or outside of your rostered event.

**Other things to note:** Meals are only for rostered musicians in your respective rooms. Spouses are welcome to join for prayer, but they have not been included in the meal count for the day.

### By Location

**515** — Auditorium, Students, and Kids teams' meals will all be served in the 515 Guest Lounge.

**CBL** — Due to the smaller spaces, band meals will be served in your respective rostered green room spaces (i.e., PK Green Room, etc.). Teams may still be invited to join in pre-gathering prayer in the CBL Green Room in the auditorium building, but outside of that, if you are rostered to serve in Kids or Students, please be mindful of staying with your respective teams in your rostered spaces.

**TRL** — Auditorium + Student + Kids meals are served in the Guest Lounge on level 4.
