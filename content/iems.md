## In-Ear Monitors (IEMs)

When playing in most of our environments, our team uses in-ear monitors. If you do not own a pair, we have a small number of universal pairs available to use. If you do own your own pair, we encourage you to bring your own IEMs when you play or sing.

If borrowing a pair from our team, you will need to trade in your car keys with the production team. Following the gatherings, simply return the in-ears and trade them back in for your car keys.

We have partnerships with 64 Audio that allow us a slight discount on their custom in-ear monitors. If you are interested in ordering a pair, please email [jd.myers@passioncitychurch.com](mailto:jd.myers@passioncitychurch.com), and he will send you pricing as well as other information to help you in the process.
