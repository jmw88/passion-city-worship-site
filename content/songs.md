## Songs

Each week you will receive the set list before the event / mid-week rehearsals. The set list is subject to change, so it would also be helpful for you and everyone you serve with if you are familiar with songs from at least the last few Passion recordings + the PCC Master Song List. Most of the songs we lead will be from this master list or found on these albums + other notable worship albums from other artists such as Hillsong, Bethel Music, Elevation Worship, etc.

For other Special Events (i.e., The Grove, YA, Fight Night, etc.), you may receive the set list somewhere in the ballpark of 2–4 days out, and on occasion, earlier / later.
