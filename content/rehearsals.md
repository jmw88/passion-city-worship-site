## Mid-Week Rehearsals

If a Sunday auditorium date comes with a rehearsal, you will be expected to be a part of our mid-week rehearsals. Rehearsal dates are attached to your PCO requests. Please be sure to look at your full availability (including the rehearsal) before accepting any dates.

For all other events, we typically do not have an additional rehearsal outside of the few hours before the event (YA, The Rising, Kids, etc.), but on the occasion we do have some extended rehearsals, we will do our best to notify you as soon as we know.
