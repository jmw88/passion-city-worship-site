## Environment / Stage Presence

In the gatherings, we are trying to create an atmosphere that reflects the joy and life of Christ. When we are celebrating, we don't want you to "perform," but we do want to encourage being expressive and engaged. We want you to be honest and genuine. The more engaged you are, the more we get to help free up others. **Free people free people!**

There will also be worshipful moments full of reverence and awe. We want you to grow to be sensitive to what the Lord is doing and help lead others to respond, all the while maintaining the balance of worshiping while leading — not being so lost in our worship that we forget to take people with us, keeping our eyes open (both spiritually and physically), and outwardly expressing what God has already done in us.
