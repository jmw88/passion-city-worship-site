## Wristbands

When serving in Passion Students, Passion Kids, or Bloom, wristbands must be worn at all times.

- We prioritize the safety of our children, so the wristband indicates your background check is up to date, on file, and that you are safe to enter.
- Please pick up your wristbands in the 515 or CBL auditorium building or TRL LIVE when you arrive. Please pick these up before or immediately following soundcheck.

### Restrooms in Kids

The restrooms in Kids and Bloom are off limits once children enter the premises. This is for the safety and protection of our children. The only times you may use the restrooms within Passion Kids or Bloom would be during non-Kids and Bloom hours.
