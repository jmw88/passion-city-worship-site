We are so excited for the opportunity to serve alongside you and look forward to getting to know you better as we all serve together. You are extremely valuable to us, and we are so grateful for the time, energy, and prayer you are dedicating to our team!

At the heart of our team is a desire to glorify God and encourage our House through worship. We believe that worship, as a lifestyle, is our response to the revelation of a holy God. When we gather, we respond to God's radical grace through extravagant worship all to the praise of His glory. We also embrace the responsibility and mantle the Lord has placed on us to be His vessels. We pray that your time on our team would spur you on further in prayer, worship, intimacy with the Father, and a growth in Christlikeness. Additionally, we pray that this team would be a family to you! There is immense value in nurturing healthy relationships with one another as God is glorified when His people are unified.

This booklet includes a directory of our staff, so you know who oversees each room and recognize our faces! We are all a resource for you, whether it is anything from technical questions to spiritual care. Additionally, the bulk of this booklet outlines detailed information regarding everything you need to know about serving on our team. Again, thank you for being a part, and if you have any questions, feel free to email [worship@passioncitychurch.com](mailto:worship@passioncitychurch.com).

What an honor it is to do what we love, serve in this capacity, and point people's eyes up to Jesus and the finished work of the cross. We are excited to see the ways God uses you to minister to our House.

Exciting days ahead!

**Jeff Johnson**
Director of Worship
