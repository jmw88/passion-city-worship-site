## Band Seating on Sundays

Our team helps lead both on and off the stage, and one of the ways we get to help lead off stage is by being present in the gatherings. Each week, if you are serving with our team on a Sunday, and unless told otherwise, please plan to sit together in at least the 9:30a gathering at all of our locations.

If you are bringing a spouse or guest, or if you plan to also attend the 11:45a gathering, please email Landon Wolfe at [landon.wolfe@passioncitychurch.com](mailto:landon.wolfe@passioncitychurch.com) so we can reserve seats for you.
