## What to Wear

We want you to be yourself. You are on a stage and in front of people, but the goal is to connect people to God, not to attract attention to yourself. If in doubt, bring some spare articles of clothing, and feel free to ask some of the leaders (same gender) or text a photo to a trusted and honest friend. Please know that our heart is for our team to have freedom to move about in such a way that your articles of clothing will not be a distraction to you or others.

### General Guidelines

**Tops / Shirts**
- Can be loose fitting — please avoid any that are tight or not loose
- Must be long enough to cover below waist / midriff
- If wearing a graphic tee, think through the graphic and what it says
- When possible, avoid trademarked sports teams, large graphics, large logos, or large brand names *(for copyright purposes, to be less distracting, etc.)*
- Avoid tank tops, low necklines, showing cleavage, and shirts that don't cover armpits + shoulders
- Ladies — avoid white/cream/light colored tops (due to lighting & projection). If directed to wear white/light, wear a dark cami or undershirt underneath.

**Bottoms**
- Jeans/slacks — presentable with no big rips or large holes
- Avoid any pants with rips above the knees
- No shorts or leggings as pants
- No short skirts / short dresses — if wearing skirts/dresses, length must be well below the knee

**Footwear**
- Sneakers are a great and comfortable option
- Avoid high heels/stilettos — mainly for your safety and comfort
